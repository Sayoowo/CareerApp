## Communication Best Practices

### With Instructor

- **Be professional**: Clear, respectful, well-organized communication
- **Be proactive**: Reach out early with questions or concerns
- **Be prepared**: Come to office hours with specific questions
- **Be responsive**: Reply promptly to instructor feedback
- **Be specific**: Include relevant links, context, and clear requests

### Weekly Reports

- Submit on time every week
- Include GitHub activity links
- Be honest about challenges
- Ask for help when needed
- Keep it concise but complete
